# Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I get a quote?**
Text a photo of your items to [970-485-8701](sms:+19704858701) or call the same
number. Estimates are free and usually same-day. See [Pricing & Estimates](/pricing.md).

**Is there a website form to book?**
No. Booking is by call or text only — it's faster and more personal. See [How to Book](/booking.md).

**Do I have to move the items outside first?**
No. The crew does **all** the loading, from wherever the items are.

**What areas do you serve?**
Summit County, Colorado — Breckenridge, Frisco, Silverthorne, Dillon, Keystone, and
Blue River. Full list on the [Service Area](/service-area.md) page.

**What can you take?**
Furniture, appliances, mattresses, yard and construction debris, hot tubs, and full
property cleanouts. The complete list is on the [Services](/services.md) page.

**Are you insured?**
Yes — Junk En Poy is fully insured.

**How much does it cost?**
It depends on volume, item type, and access. You get an up-front quote before any work
starts, with no hidden fees. See [Pricing & Estimates](/pricing.md).

**How soon can you come?**
We reply fast — often same-day — and schedule pickups quickly. Call or text
[970-485-8701](tel:+19704858701) to check availability.
