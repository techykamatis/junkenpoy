# Pricing & Estimates

Junk En Poy gives **free, no-obligation estimates**. There's no web form — the fastest
way to a price is to text a photo.

## Get a price in three steps

1. **Snap a photo** of the items you want removed.
2. **Text it to [970-485-8701](sms:+19704858701)** (or call to describe the job).
3. **Get a quote** — we usually reply the same day with fair, up-front pricing.

## What affects the price

* **Volume** — how much space the items take up in the truck.
* **Item type** — bulky or heavy items (hot tubs, appliances) versus light clutter.
* **Access** — stairs, tight spaces, or long carries.
* **Disposal** — special handling for certain materials.

Pricing is always **up-front** — you approve the quote before we haul anything, and
there are no surprise fees.
